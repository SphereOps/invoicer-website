# Features Overview

Invoicer comes packed with features designed to make invoice management simple and efficient.

## Core Features

### Self-Hosted Architecture

Complete control over your data. Deploy on your own infrastructure with Docker. No cloud dependencies, no data sharing with third parties.

### Professional Templates

Beautiful, customizable invoice templates that look professional and polished. Choose from multiple templates or create your own.

### REST API

Full-featured REST API for programmatic invoice generation and management. Integrate Invoicer into your existing workflow.

### PDF Export

Generate high-quality PDF invoices ready for printing or email delivery. All PDFs are generated server-side for optimal quality.

### Easy Setup

Get up and running in minutes with Docker. No complex configuration required. Just pull the image and run.

### Customizable

Customize invoice templates, branding, and fields to match your business needs. Full control over the invoice format and appearance.

## Advanced Features

### Invoice Status Tracking

Track invoices through their lifecycle: draft, sent, paid, overdue. Get notifications and reminders.

### Client Management

Store client information for quick invoice creation. Manage contact details, addresses, and payment terms.

### Tax Calculations

Automatic tax calculations with support for multiple tax rates. Handle VAT, sales tax, and custom tax rules.

### Multi-Currency Support

Create invoices in multiple currencies. Automatic currency formatting and conversion.

### Search and Filter

Powerful search and filtering capabilities. Find invoices by number, client, date range, or status.
